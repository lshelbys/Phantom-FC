Phantom FC Win the Dumankaya Cup Final 7–4

8 August 2025 • Phantom FC
DUMANKAYA CUP CHAMPIONS

Phantom FC are Dumankaya Cup champions after a stunning 7–4 victory over Shabab Al Hura in the final at Dumankaya Street Stadium on Friday 8 August 2025. Tayem Eyad was the standout performer, netting four goals in a breathtaking display.

The final was anything but straightforward. Shabab Al Hura drew first blood in the opening minute through Giyath, but Phantom FC responded immediately — Tayem Eyad equalised at 2' and added a second just a minute later to put the side ahead 2–1.

Samo pulled Shabab Al Hura level at 12' to make it 2–2, but Phantom FC hit back hard. Jasem scored at 13' to restore the lead, Tayem added a fourth at 14', and with the score at 4–2, the trophy looked within reach.

Shabab Al Hura refused to go quietly. Samo struck twice in quick succession at 15' and 16' to bring the deficit back to just one goal — 4–3, then 5–4 — setting up a tense final stretch. But Phantom FC held their nerve. Jasem extended the lead to 5–3 at 15', then 6–4 at 17', before Tayem sealed an unforgettable win in stoppage time to make it 7–4.

"This is what we work for. We showed real character tonight — they came back at us twice and we responded both times. This trophy belongs to every player who has put in the work for this club."
