Almail SC Renews Amateur Club Status for 2025

17 June 2026 • Almail Sports Club
ALMAIL SPORTS CLUB

Almail Sports Club is proud to announce the renewal of its amateur club status for the 2025 season, reaffirming the club's commitment to grassroots football and the development of talent within the community.

The renewal of amateur status marks another important milestone for Almail SC as the club continues to build its identity and structure in competitive amateur football. Since its founding, the club has prioritised fair play, community involvement, and player development — values that remain central to everything the organisation stands for.

Amateur status allows Almail SC to participate in registered competitions while maintaining the core principles of the sport at grassroots level. The club has been active in the local football community and most recently captured the Dumankaya Cup in August 2025, defeating Shabab Al Hura 7-4 in a memorable final at Dumankaya Street Stadium.

"Renewing our amateur club status is not just a formality — it is a reaffirmation of who we are and what we stand for. We are building this club the right way, from the ground up."

Almail Sports Club

The club is currently focused on expanding its squad ahead of the upcoming season. Players interested in joining Almail SC are encouraged to reach out directly to the club with their details including name, position, date of birth, country, height, and a player photo.

Looking ahead, Almail SC is committed to participating in further competitions, developing its home at Bizarri's Stadium, and growing its supporter base across the region and online.

All supporters are encouraged to follow the club across social media for the latest news, match updates, and club announcements.
